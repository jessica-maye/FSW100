I will blog my life away


What I remember first was, telling my mom "No I Was."

Then my mom invited my sister in the room, to force the truth.

After that, I was not able to see my grandma.

Maybe it was a couple months

But when I did, she looked sick. She was crying and drained.

It was my brother's football game and she was standing by a tree.

No one else was around and she wanted to run to me, I could tell.

"Jessica, tell me what happened," she whispered out.

I was thinking, "how could you now know?" But then again how would anyone?
